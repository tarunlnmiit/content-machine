## How to use

1. Go to Canva AI 2.0
2. Choose template above matching blog niche
3. Replace bracketed values with actual blog data
4. Paste into Canva AI chat
5. Wait for AI to generate all 3 assets
6. Review + export:
   - Thumbnail → `assets/thumbnails/{slug}_thumb.png`
   - Slides → `assets/{slug}_slides.pdf`
   - Quote card → `assets/quote_cards/{slug}_quote.png`

---

## What happens with Memory Library

- AI remembers your niche-specific styles
- Auto-applies brand colors
- Generates fully editable, layered output (not flat images)
- Change individual elements without regenerating entire design
- Consistent styling across all outputs
