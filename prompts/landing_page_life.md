# Life & Self-Dev Worksheet Landing Page — Claude Design Prompt

Create a warm, approachable landing page for a Life & Self-Development worksheet lead magnet using the brand kit below.

## Brand Kit

- **Background**: Dark Navy (#1a1a2e)
- **Text/Primary**: Cream (#c9c4b8)
- **Accent 1**: Muted Blue (#7896a8)
- **Accent 2**: Gold (#a89968)
- **Pop/CTA**: Coral (#e07856)

## Page Structure

### Hero Section (50% of page)
- Background: Dark Navy with subtle texture (calm, grounding feel)
- Headline (80pt, Cream): "Design Your Check-in for Your Worst Day"
- Subheadline (32pt, Gold): "A 5-minute routine that works"
- Body text (16pt, Cream): "Stop spiraling. Start building. Get the worksheet that helps you stay grounded when everything feels off. Used by 5,000+ people navigating life's tough moments."

### Visual Element
- Centered illustration: Warm, abstract pattern (circles, soft shapes) using Gold + Muted Blue
- Evoke calm, reflection, personal growth
- Subtle background warmth: Dark Navy to very subtle warm undertone

### Form Section (40% of page)
- Background: Slightly warmer navy (#332f3f with warm tone)
- Headline (40pt, Cream): "Get Your Free Worksheet"
- Body text (14pt, Gold): "5 journal prompts + check-in template + daily reminder card"
- **[CONVERTKIT EMBED PLACEHOLDER]** — embed ConvertKit form here (form title: "Download Your Worksheet", CTA button color: Coral)
- Form fields: Name + Email only
- Small reassurance text (12pt, Muted Blue): "We respect your inbox. Unsubscribe anytime."

### Footer Section (10% of page)
- Background: Gold (#a89968)
- Text (14pt, Dark Navy): "By [Your Name] • Wellness + self-development insights • Join 5,000+ readers"
- Warm, inviting tone

## Design Specifications

- **Layout**: Single-column, centered, A4 or web-optimized
- **Typography**: Warm sans-serif (human, approachable) or serif headlines (sophisticated but warm)
- **Spacing**: Generous padding (40px margins), calm and spacious
- **Style**: Minimalist but warm, inviting without being cutesy
- **Button**: Coral CTA button with rounded corners (8px), white text, 16pt bold
- **Color balance**: Use Gold sparingly but warmly; let Cream + Navy dominate
- **Accessibility**: High contrast, readable on mobile and desktop

## Tone & Copy

- Personal, relatable language
- Validate struggle: "when everything feels off"
- Offer concrete value: "5-minute routine", "5 prompts"
- Build community: "5,000+ people"
- Reassuring, not pushy

## Notes

- Lead with the transformation/benefit (spiraling → grounded)
- Use warmth and community to encourage signups
- ConvertKit form should match brand warmth
- No animations; clean, calm, focused design
- This is a safe space—design should feel like a hug
