# Poetry Community Landing Page — Claude Design Prompt

Create an elegant, introspective landing page for poetry community/newsletter signup using the brand kit below.

## Brand Kit

- **Background**: Dark Navy (#1a1a2e)
- **Text/Primary**: Cream (#c9c4b8)
- **Accent 1**: Muted Blue (#7896a8)
- **Accent 2**: Gold (#a89968)
- **Pop/CTA**: Coral (#e07856)

## Page Structure

### Hero Section (50% of page)
- Background: Dark Navy with subtle vertical line patterns (literary, contemplative)
- Headline (72pt, Cream, italic or elegant serif): "Poetry That Mirrors Your Inner Landscape"
- Subheadline (28pt, Gold): "Weekly reflections on love, loss, and becoming"
- Body text (16pt, Cream): "Join a community of 3,000+ readers discovering poetry that speaks to the soul. One new poem + reflection every Thursday."

### Visual Element
- Centered poetic graphic: Abstract flowing lines or constellation-like pattern using Gold + Muted Blue
- Evoke emotion, depth, introspection
- Subtle motion implied through design (but static in final design)

### Form Section (40% of page)
- Background: Dark Navy (slightly elevated, shadowed)
- Headline (40pt, Cream): "Join the Thursday Ritual"
- Body text (14pt, Gold): "New poem + 3 reflection prompts + subscriber-only archive"
- **[CONVERTKIT EMBED PLACEHOLDER]** — embed ConvertKit form here (form title: "Subscribe to Weekly Poetry", CTA button color: Coral)
- Form fields: Name + Email only
- Poetic reassurance text (12pt, Muted Blue, italicized): "Your inbox is a sacred space. Unsubscribe anytime."

### Testimonial or Quote Section (Optional, 10% of page)
- Background: Muted Blue (#7896a8)
- Include one subscriber testimonial or featured poem excerpt (12-16pt, Cream)
- Italic, left-aligned, minimal attribution

### Footer Section (10% of page)
- Background: Gold (#a89968)
- Text (14pt, Dark Navy): "By [Your Name] • Poetry • Wellness • Human connection | Join 3,000+ readers"
- Literary, understated elegance

## Design Specifications

- **Layout**: Single-column, centered, A4 or web-optimized
- **Typography**: Elegant serif fonts (headings) + clean sans-serif (body) for contrast
- **Spacing**: Ample whitespace; poetry requires breathing room
- **Style**: Sophisticated, literary, minimalist, introspective
- **Button**: Coral CTA button with rounded corners (8px), white text, 16pt bold
- **Color balance**: Navy as base, Gold as accent warmth, Muted Blue for depth
- **Accessibility**: High contrast, readable on mobile and desktop, test on dark mode

## Tone & Copy

- Literary, poetic language ("inner landscape", "sacred space", "mirrors")
- Emotional resonance (loss, becoming, love, reflection)
- Community-focused ("3,000+ readers", "join a community")
- Thursday ritual emphasis (consistency, mindfulness)
- Vulnerable, authentic tone—poetry is intimate

## Visual Metaphors

- Flowing lines (river, time, emotion)
- Constellations (meaning, connection, guided contemplation)
- Vertical lines (pages, books, poetry structure)
- Negative space (introspection, silence, depth)

## Notes

- Lead with emotion and belonging, not transactional value
- Poetry is intimate—design should feel safe, meditative
- ConvertKit form should integrate seamlessly (consider dark form design)
- No animations; stillness is poetic
- Every element should feel intentional, not decorative
- Test readability with poetic language; avoid clutter
