## How to use

**Canva Bulk Create Workflow:**

1. Open Slide Deck brand template in Canva
2. Apps → Bulk Create
3. Upload `output/scheduled/{slug}_slides.csv`
4. **For hero slide:** Use corresponding HERO prompt in Canva AI Text to Image
5. **For content slides:** Use corresponding SUBSLIDE prompt in Canva AI Text to Image
6. Map CSV columns: `title → heading`, `bullet1/2/3 → bullet zones`
7. Generate → apply Magic Animate (Breathe or Fade)
8. Export PDF → `assets/{slug}_slides.pdf`

## Visual Style Guide by Domain

**Data Science/Tech**
- Digital art → **3D model, Concept art**
- **Neon** (tech aesthetic) or **Gradient** (computational/modern)
- Avoid photography/fine art

**Life & Self-Dev**
- Photography → **Filmic, Soft focus, Minimalist**
- **Moody** (emotional depth)
- **Photo** (authentic/relatable)
- Warm/human-centered

**Poetry & Quotes**
- Fine art → **Watercolor, Oil painting, Ink print**
- **Stained glass** (luminous/contemplative)
- **Papercut** (delicate/artistic)
- Also: **Dreamy, Moody** from upper sections

**Rule:** Don't mix categories. Tech ≠ photography. Poetry ≠ 3D.
