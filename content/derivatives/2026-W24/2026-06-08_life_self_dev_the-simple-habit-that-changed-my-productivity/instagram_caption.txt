The most productive people I know don't have complicated systems.

They have a handful of small habits they actually do.

I spent years building productivity systems that eventually collapsed under their own weight.

Then I stripped back to 7 habits simple enough to survive my worst days.

The shutdown ritual. The weekly brain dump. One frog. The done list. One 90-minute block. Tomorrow's three priorities. One metric.

None require an app. Most take under five minutes.
