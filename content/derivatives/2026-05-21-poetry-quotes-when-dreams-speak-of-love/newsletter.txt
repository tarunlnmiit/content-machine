Subject: Safe and Alive: Stop Choosing
Preview: The person you're meant to love does both. Here's what that looks like.

There's a moment in loving someone when you stop protecting them from your life and start wanting to weave them into it. All at once.

A new poem explores this: the instant you imagine them being loved by your people. Sitting with your mama in the darkness and warmth. Being fully themselves without performing. That's when you melt. Out of love and the relief of finally being whole.

Safety alone is incomplete. So is aliveness without safety. But the person who gives you both? They don't ask you to choose. They collapse all your circles into one.
