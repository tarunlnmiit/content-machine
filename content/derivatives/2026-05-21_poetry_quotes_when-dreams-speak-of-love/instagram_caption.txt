Safe or alive? Choose both.

Everyone says find someone who makes you feel safe. Or alive. They're wrong.

The real love? The person who does both. Your nervous system relaxes while your presence expands. You stop performing and start belonging.

It's the moment you imagine them with your mama. Sitting in the warmth and darkness. Being fully themselves in your world. And you watching it all happen.

That's when you melt. Out of sheer love and the relief of finally being whole.
