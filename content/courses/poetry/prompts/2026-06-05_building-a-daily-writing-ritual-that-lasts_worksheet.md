## 3. Your First Honest Line (Breaking In)

Most people wait to feel inspired before they write. This prompt skips that.

Complete this sentence *without thinking for more than 10 seconds*:

> "Honestly, lately I've been feeling like ___________________________

> ________________________________________________________________"

Don't fix it. Don't make it poetic. That sentence, as-is, is the seed of a poem.
