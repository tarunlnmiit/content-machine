## Closing Self-Check

Read back your 2-minute check-in from Part 2, Prompt 5.

Ask yourself:

- Does it feel like *mine*, or does it feel like something I copied from advice I've read?
- Could I actually do this on a day when I'm exhausted and behind on assignments?

If your honest answer to either question is "not really" — revise one step right now. That's the work.
