## Section 3 — From Goal to System (5 Prompts)

Work through these one at a time. Each prompt builds on the previous one.

**Prompt 1 — Strip the outcome language**

Rewrite your goal as a *behavior* instead of a result. Not "get fit" but "do 20 minutes of movement." Not "study for NEET" but "solve 15 MCQs." What is the smallest, repeatable action that actually moves this goal?

**Prompt 4 — Set a floor, not a ceiling**

Most systems collapse because we aim too high on bad days. What is the *minimum* version of this behavior — the version so small you'd feel embarrassed skipping it?

Example: If the full behavior is "study for 60 minutes," the floor might be "open the book and do 5 problems."

## Section 4 — Write the System in One Paragraph

Combine what you learned above into a single clear system description. Fill in the blanks:

> Every **_______________** (days of week), at **_______________** (time), I will **_______________** (the behavior) for at least **_______________** (floor duration or quantity). Before I start, I will **_______________** (one prep step that removes friction). I will count the week as successful if I complete this **_______________** times.

## Closing Check

Before you close this worksheet, answer these three questions honestly:

Yes / No — If no, go back to Section 4 and add one more detail.
