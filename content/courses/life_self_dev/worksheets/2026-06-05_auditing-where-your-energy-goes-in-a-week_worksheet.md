## Closing Self-Check

Before you close this worksheet, answer these three questions quickly — gut response, no overthinking:

1. Did anything surprise you in this audit? ____

2. What do you now know for certain that you were only guessing at before? ____

3. On a scale of 1–10, how honest were you in filling this out? ____

If you wrote anything below 7 for that last one — go back to the section that felt uncomfortable. That's usually where the real drain is hiding.
