## Closing Self-Check

Before you close this worksheet, answer these three questions out loud or in writing:

1. What is the one pressure I will take action on this week? → ____

2. What is the one pressure I am choosing *not* to chase this week? → ____

3. How will I know at the end of the week if I actually followed through? → ____

---

*You do not need to resolve everything. You need to know which things are yours to carry and which things you have been carrying for no reason.*
