# Systems for Humans: Build a Life That Doesn't Exhaust You

**Niche flag:** `life` · **Audience:** All students, 16–25
**Format:** Talking-head video (8–12 min) + journaling prompts + reflection exercises · **Certificate:** Yes
**Funnel:** @breathoflife_ + Substack → course · **Launch price:** ₹149 (later ₹499–₹799)

**Angle:** A data scientist applies analytical thinking to his own life. Not
productivity hacks. Not therapy. Systems that actually stick.

> All lessons ORIGINAL. No repurposing of blogs/YouTube scripts. Separate product line.

## Modules (6–8 lessons — Tarun owns final list)

| # | Lesson | Key points (seed for `draft_lesson_script.py`) | Worksheet objective |
|---|--------|-----------------------------------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Self-audit — where is your energy going? | Measure before you fix · energy ≠ time · find the leaks | Map a week's energy and name the top 2 drains |
| 2 | Habit design — the science + the feeling | Cue→routine→reward · make it small · design the environment | Design one keystone habit with a trigger |
| 3 | Mental health maintenance, not crisis management | Daily upkeep beats rescue · early signals · openness over stigma | Build a 2-minute daily check-in |
| 4 | Dealing with pressure (academic + family + career) | Separate signal from noise · what you control · boundaries | Sort current pressures into control/no-control |
| 5 | Building review loops (weekly, monthly) | What gets reviewed gets better · light cadence · adjust, don't restart | Set up a 10-minute weekly review |
| 6 | Attention in a distracting world | Design the environment, not willpower · phone/social systems · protect one deep block | Build one distraction-free block into your day |
| 7 | Systems over goals — the long game | Identity > outcome · survive bad days · become the kind of person, don't chase the win | Rewrite one goal as a repeatable system |
