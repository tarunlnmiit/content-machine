## Tips for Using Tags

- **Use all 5 tags** per article (Medium's maximum)
- **Mix broad + niche** — e.g. `Technology` + `iOS Development`
- **Top Writer status** is awarded per tag when you publish consistently under it
- Tags with high followers but fewer stories (like `Math`, `True Crime`) offer less competition for Top Writer badges
