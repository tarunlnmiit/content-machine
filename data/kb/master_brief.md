## 📈 Platform Strategy Recommendations

### 📱 YouTube (Video Focus)
*   **Strategy:** Utilize a "Hub & Spoke" model.
*   **Hub (Long-Form):** Deep dives into Philosophy/Mindset (e.g., "Stoicism for Modern Productivity"). (Goal: Authority & Watch Time)
*   **Spokes (Short-Form):** Snippets from the Hub, or standalone, highly actionable 30-60 second tips addressing common pain points. (Goal: Reach & Discovery)

### 📝 Blog/Newsletter (Text Focus)
*   **Strategy:** The SEO and Conversion Engine.
*   **Content:** Repurpose long-form YouTube scripts into detailed, easily scannable blog posts. The newsletter acts as the **curator**, sending out the *best* tip/prompt from the week, driving traffic back to the blog/video.

### 🎙️ Podcast (Audio Focus)
*   **Strategy:** The Connection Builder (Best for Pillar 3).
*   **Content:** Interviewing guests who are successful in creative fields or who practice deep reflection. Low barrier to entry for the creator.
