She said

you made my heart feel safe,

but you make it feel alive.

A conversation held

in the darkness of the night

under the warmth of a blanket

over the clouds in dreamland

around a bonfire

with mama

She said

I replied, “You don’t even know who it is.”

A joy blossomed across her face

and uttered

the sweetest symphony entered my ears

The twinkle in your eyes

can rival

that of a star

when you talk about her

I melted right then and there

The only wish I have now is

To see this dream become a reality

To sit and observe you having

Such conversations with mama

Then dissolving in my arms

out of sheer love and embarrassment

***— Carved in the silence between heartbeats — Tarun***
